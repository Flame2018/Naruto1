Preadolescence



Background

My story is pretty complicated actually. So in every village there dwells a tailed beast which is sealed within a chosen shinobi granting them loads of both chackra and power. In my village, we harbored the nine-tailed fox who was set free on the village destroying everything in his path. The Fourth Hokage at the time held off the beast and sealed it in a child to defeat it. In the process of him saving the village he and his wife died. As far as myself, my parents died in the nine tails attack leaving me all alone. Growing up, people always distanced themselves from me and that made me act out and fail the academy multiple times just to get some attention. Then when I failed again because I couldn’t execute shadow clone jutsu I just was going to give up until someone gave me a scroll containing our village secrets egging me on to run away with it. All other shinobi were sent to retrieve me and the scroll. Iruka Sensei found me practicing a jutsu within the scroll and things escalated quickly once the man who gave me the scroll came also. Long story short, the man who gave me the scroll for his own intentions was a traitor who tried to kill both Iruka Sensei and I. During that time, he told me that the nine-tailed fox was sealed within me, but that didn’t matter for my sensei because he believed in me, and that was enough. From then on, I told myself I'd become a hokage of the village one day so everyone will look up to me and respect me. BELIEVE IT.
